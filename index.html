We GotchU

GotchU is the easiest way to cure hangryness. We find the best restaurant for you based on your preferences. No more scouring through reviews and searching the web. Coming soon to iOS and Android.

Step 1. Set up your profile.

Step 2. When you're ready to go, select your in-the-moment preferences and we'll do the rest.

About Us

We, like many of you suffer from “hangryness”, caused by our indecisive nature and access to an abundance of information. We make the restaurant selection simple for diners with curated data and learned preferences. Eliminate “hangryness”, save friendships and build stronger relationships… we Gotchu.
