We GotchU

GotchU is the easiest way to cure hangryness. We find the best restaurant for you based on your preferences. No more scouring through reviews and searching the web. Coming soon to iOS and Android.

Easy Profile Setup

Sign up using Facebook or Google. Fill out your profile just one time and we'll save your preferences to curate the best restaurants for you.

Painless Decision Making

You've already filled out a profile, so let us do the rest. Each time you use the app, just pick your prefered spending limit, ETA and the type of meal you're in the mood for. We pick the best restaurant for you so you don't have to decide. We even recommend a dish to try out.

Convenient Integrations

Connects directly to Google Maps for directions. Planning a date? Easily make a res with Open Table. Ordering take-out? Integrated with Seamless and GrubHub. Need a Lyft? We GotchU covered.
